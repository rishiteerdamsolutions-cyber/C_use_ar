Shipping & Delivery

Last updated: 23 April 2026

cusear™ primarily delivers digital goods and services (software, API keys, hosted portals, and prepaid usage credits). This policy describes how and when delivery occurs. There is no physical shipment unless we expressly offer a physical product in writing for your order.

1. Desktop application

After successful payment (where a download is part of your purchase), download links and any licence or activation instructions are provided:

  • On the order confirmation screen, where shown; and/or
  • By email to the address you supplied at checkout; and/or
  • Inside your customer account or dashboard, when applicable.

Delivery is typically immediate or within minutes of payment confirmation. Rare delays may occur due to payment verification, fraud checks, or email filtering — check spam folders or contact support if access does not arrive within 24 hours.

2. API keys and prepaid credits

API keys, credit balances, and entitlements are activated on our systems after payment confirmation. You can usually begin calling the API or using metered features as soon as your dashboard shows an active key and positive balance.

3. White-label and hosted portals

Provisioning a branded subdomain, DNS instructions, or admin access may take up to 1–2 business days after we receive a completed order and any required branding assets. We will communicate expected timelines if automation cannot complete setup instantly.

4. “Shipping” address

We may collect a billing or business address for tax, invoicing, or fraud prevention. This is not used for courier delivery unless you have purchased an explicitly physical item.

5. Failed delivery

If you did not receive access you paid for, contact aideveloperindia@gmail.com or WhatsApp +91 95050 09699 with your order reference. We will resend credentials or restore access after verification.

6. Regional availability

Some payment methods or features may be limited by geography or regulation. Checkout will indicate if a product is unavailable in your region.
