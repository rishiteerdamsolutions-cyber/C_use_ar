Pricing FAQ

Do credits expire?
No. Credits you purchase never expire and roll over indefinitely.
What happens when I run out of credits?
API calls return a 402 error. Top up instantly from your dashboard or the billing API — no downtime.
Can I cancel the white-label plan?
Yes, cancel anytime. Your portal stays active until the end of the billing month. No lock-in.
Is GST included?
All prices shown are exclusive of GST. 18% GST will be added at checkout as required by Indian tax law.
Which payment methods are accepted?
UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular wallets via Razorpay. All transactions in INR.
Can I get a refund?
Unused credits can be refunded within 7 days of purchase. Monthly plans are non-refundable after the portal is provisioned. Contact support@yourplatform.com.
