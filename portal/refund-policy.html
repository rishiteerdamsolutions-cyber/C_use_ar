Refund Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

This Refund Policy explains when you may receive a refund for purchases made through cusear™. It should be read together with our Terms & Conditions and Shipping & Delivery policy.

1. Credit packs (one-time purchases)

If you purchased prepaid credits (“credit packs”) and have not materially used those credits, you may request a full refund within 7 calendar days of the original payment date. “Materially used” means consumption of credits for successful API calls, workflow runs, or other metered actions as shown in your account usage logs.

After 7 days, or once credits have been materially used, credit pack purchases are non-refundable, except where required by applicable law or at our sole discretion in cases of proven billing error or service-wide outage not attributable to your misuse.

2. Subscriptions (including white-label)

Monthly or recurring subscription fees are generally non-refundable once the billing period has started and access (for example, portal provisioning, included credits, or hosted features) has been activated.

If you cancel a subscription, you typically retain access until the end of the current paid period; we do not provide partial refunds for unused days within that period unless required by law or agreed in writing.

If we terminate your access for a reason other than your breach of the Terms, we will provide a pro-rata refund of prepaid unused fees for the affected period, where applicable.

3. Desktop software / licence fees

Where a separate desktop licence or per-delivery fee applies, refund eligibility depends on what was delivered:

  • If you did not receive download access or a valid licence key due to a technical fault on our side, contact support within 7 days for a correction or refund.
  • If you successfully downloaded and activated the software, refunds follow the same 7-day window for unused entitlements only, subject to verification.

4. How to request a refund

Email aideveloperindia@gmail.com or WhatsApp +91 95050 09699 with your registered email, order or transaction ID, date of purchase, and reason for the request. We aim to respond within 5 business days.

5. Processing time and method

Approved refunds are sent to the original payment method where technically possible. Depending on your bank or card issuer, posting may take 5–14 business days. UPI refunds may appear sooner but are still subject to payment-partner timelines.

6. Chargebacks

Please contact us before initiating a chargeback or payment dispute so we can resolve the issue. Abusive or fraudulent chargebacks may result in account closure and recovery of fees where permitted.

7. Changes

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will change accordingly. Purchases are governed by the policy in effect at the time of purchase unless a newer policy expressly improves your rights.
